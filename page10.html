Funding

Our funding is from a variety of sources including Canadian Institutes of Health Research (CIHR), Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council (NSERC), Canadian Foundation for Innovation (CFI), Ontario Government, Roche Canada, FastGrants, SickKids Hospital, Ontario Lung Association, and others. In addition to awarded funding, our Team is actively applying to these and other agencies to support and grow the Hirota Lung Health Lab.

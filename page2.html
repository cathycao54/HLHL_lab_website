Quynh (Cathy) Cao
PhD Student

Cathy is a PhD student in the Hirota Lab. She completed her undergraduate degree at the University of Toronto where she with Dr. Theo Moraes studying the effects of Nigericin and Diazoxide on HEp-2 cells infected with RSV in vitro. She came to McMaster University for her graduate studies where she started her MSc in the Hirota Lab before transferring to the PhD stream. Cathy will be investigating and characterizing ABCF1 as a novel antiviral sensor in human airway epithelial cells. 

During her free time, Cathy enjoys playing video games with her friends, practicing calligraphy, drinking coffee and playing with her two adorable (yet troublesome) cats.

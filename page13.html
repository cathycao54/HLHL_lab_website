Quynh (Cathy) Cao

PhD Student

Cathy completed her undergraduate degree at the University of Toronto. She came to McMaster University for her graduate studies where she started her MSc in the HLHL before transferring to the PhD stream. Cathy will be investigating and characterizing ABCF1 as a novel antiviral sensor in human airway epithelial cells. During her free time, Cathy enjoys playing video games, practicing calligraphy, drinking coffee and playing with her two adorable (yet troublesome) cats.
